Lucía Pancorbo Fernández

MSc Medical Imaging and AI @ ETH Zürich

Zürich, Switzerland

I am Lucía, a MSc student in Biomedica Engineering at ETH Zürich fully funded by ‘la Caixa’ foundation and supported by the ‘Rafael del Pino’ foundation. I hold a BSc in Biomedical Engineering from Carlos III University of Madrid (2nd in my cohort), with an exchange at Nanyang Technological University of Singapore.

My journey in computational research is driven by a passion for developing Machine Learning and Computer Vision frameworks to solve complex challenges in healthcare. I have recently completed two research stays at Harvard Medical School, specifically within the Mahmood Lab and the Athinoula A. Martinos Center for Biomedical Imaging.

My interests lie in AI for Healthcare, with a focus on Computer Vision, Medical Image Analysis and Data Science.
